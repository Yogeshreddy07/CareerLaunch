Why We Exist

Building a Future Where Every Woman Can Lead

Pinky Tech was born from a simple yet powerful realization: talent is universal, but opportunity is not. Too many brilliant women step back from their careers due to family responsibilities, only to find the doors closed when they are ready to return.

We exist to reopen those doors. Whether you are a fresh graduate looking for direction or a professional returning after a break, we provide the ecosystem, training, and confidence you need to stand tall in the tech industry.

This isn't just a training institute; it's a movement to empower women with financial independence and professional pride.

Our Purpose

Driven by Impact

Our goals are clear, ambitious, and centered around your success.

Our Mission

To empower women with industry-relevant skills, confidence, and direct placement opportunities, ensuring financial independence and professional dignity. We strive to remove the barriers of career gaps and lack of guidance.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in women's tech empowerment, creating a world where women have equal representation, leadership opportunities, and the freedom to build successful careers on their own terms.

The Challenge

The Career Gap Barrier

Returning to work after a break is intimidating. Technology changes fast, and confidence can fade. Many women feel their skills are outdated or that the industry has moved on without them.

Traditional generic training institutes often fail to address these specific anxieties and gaps, leaving women feeling unsupported.

The Pinky Tech Solution

We don't just teach code. We rebuild confidence, update your tech stack, provide mock corporate environments, and hand-hold you until you sign your offer letter.

Community & Mentorship

You are never alone. Our community of mentors and peers ensures that whenever you feel stuck, there is a hand ready to pull you up.
